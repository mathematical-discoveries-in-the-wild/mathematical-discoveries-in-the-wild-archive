# Partial packet: dimension-two operator-valued frame potential

Status: `candidate_partial_likely_valid`

Source target: Tom Needham and Clayton Shonkwiler, "Fusion Frame Homotopy and Tightening Fusion Frames by Gradient Descent", arXiv:2208.11045.

Extracted conjecture: Conjecture 5.1 asks whether, for fixed spectral data `r`, all local minima of the generalized operator-valued frame potential `OFP` on `OF(r)` are global minima.

Result: The conjecture holds in Hilbert dimension `d=2`, over both the real and complex fields, for arbitrary finite fixed spectral data. The proof reduces every fixed-spectrum two-dimensional Hermitian orbit to a weighted unit sphere and proves that the weighted vector-sum energy has no non-global local minima.
