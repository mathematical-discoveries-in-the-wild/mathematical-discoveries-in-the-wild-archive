## Historical partial result

The initial promoted packet proved the following concrete subcase of Section 6 Question 1.

Let `X = ell_2`, let `W_lambda` be `lambda` times the backward shift for some `lambda > 1`, and let `N` be any nonzero nilpotent operator on `X`. Then, for every separable admissible Banach ideal `J subset L(X)`, the generalized derivation

```text
tau_{W_lambda,N}(S) = W_lambda S - S N
```

satisfies the Hypercyclicity Criterion on `J`, hence is hypercyclic.
