# Literature Status: The `C[0,1]` pre-Komlos question from arXiv:1509.07914

Status: `literature_already_answered`

Source/open-problem paper: N. Gao, V. G. Troitsky, and F. Xanthos, "Uo-convergence and its applications to Cesaro means in Banach lattices", arXiv:1509.07914.

Target question: after Example 5.3, the source paper says:

> We do not know whether `C[0,1]` has the pre-Komlos property, or more generally, when `C(K)` has the pre-Komlos property.

Supporting answer: E. Y. Emelyanov, N. Erkursun-Ozcan, and S. G. Gorokhova, "Komlos properties in Banach lattices", arXiv:1710.02580.

The supporting paper explicitly identifies the source terminology: its `nuo`-pre-Komlos property is the pre-Komlos property of Gao--Troitsky--Xanthos. In Section 3 it then says that the `C[0,1]` case was unknown in arXiv:1509.07914, and proves that `C(K)` fails the relevant pre-Komlos property whenever `K` is a compact metric space possessing a nonempty separable open subset without isolated points. Since `K=[0,1]` satisfies this hypothesis, `C[0,1]` does not have the pre-Komlos property.

## Scope

This packet records an already-known literature answer, not a new counterexample from this run. It answers the explicit `C[0,1]` question and gives a broad negative class of compact metric examples. It does not classify all compact Hausdorff spaces `K` for which `C(K)` has the pre-Komlos property.
