# LUR quasihyperbolic balls: explicitly answered in later literature

**Status:** `literature_already_answered`.

Klén, Rasila, and Talponen ask on page 15 of arXiv:1407.2403 whether
quasihyperbolic balls centered at the origin in symmetric convex domains of
reflexive LUR Banach spaces induce LUR norms by their Minkowski functionals.

Rasila, Talponen, and Zhang explicitly identify and affirmatively answer this
question in Theorem 3.2 on page 5 of arXiv:1708.02240. Their abstract also says
that the paper answers the earlier question posed with R. Klén. The theorem
proves a stronger sequential LUR statement and concludes that every centered
quasihyperbolic ball in a symmetric convex domain induces an equivalent LUR
norm.

This is an author-acknowledged exact later answer, not a new run result.
