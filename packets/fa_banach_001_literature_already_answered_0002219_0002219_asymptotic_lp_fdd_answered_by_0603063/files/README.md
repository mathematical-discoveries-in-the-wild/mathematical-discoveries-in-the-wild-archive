# Asymptotic ell_p FDD embedding problem from arXiv:math/0002219

Status: `literature_already_answered`

Source/open-problem paper: Edward Odell and Thomas Schlumprecht,
*Trees and Branches in Banach Spaces*, arXiv:math/0002219.

Supporting answer paper: Edward Odell, Thomas Schlumprecht, and Andras Zsak,
*On the structure of asymptotic l_p spaces*, arXiv:math/0603063.

## Identification

Problem 5.4 of arXiv:math/0002219 asks whether every reflexive asymptotic
`ell_p` space, for `1<p<infty`, embeds into a space with an asymptotic `ell_p`
finite-dimensional decomposition.

arXiv:math/0603063 explicitly answers this.  Its abstract states that every
separable reflexive asymptotic `ell_p` space embeds into a reflexive space with
an asymptotic `ell_p` FDD.  More directly, the end of Section 4 says that a
special case of Corollary 4.8 solves Problem 5.4 from the earlier Odell-
Schlumprecht paper, and Corollary 4.9 states the embedding result for reflexive
asymptotic `ell_p` spaces.

## Scope

This packet records an already-known positive answer to Problem 5.4 as stated
in the source.  The supporting theorem is stronger than the source question:
it gives a reflexive containing space, includes the quotient formulation, and
covers the customary endpoint notation as well.  This packet does not claim a
new proof or any fresh strengthening.
