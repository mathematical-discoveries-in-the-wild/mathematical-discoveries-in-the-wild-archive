Relevant abstract content:

If `G` is a countable discrete group with the Haagerup-Kraus approximation
property and `VN(G)` has QWEP, then `L_p(VN(G))` has a very nice local
structure, is a `COL_p` space, and has a completely bounded Schauder basis.
