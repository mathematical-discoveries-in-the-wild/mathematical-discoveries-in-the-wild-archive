# Literature Already Answered: two Bishop-property questions

Status: `literature_already_answered` for two questions from arXiv:1211.2770.

Source/open-problem paper:

- Klaas Pieter Hart, Tomasz Kania, and Tomasz Kochanek, "A chain condition for operators from C(K)-spaces", arXiv:1211.2770.

Supporting answer paper:

- Tomasz Kania and Richard J. Smith, "Chains of functions in C(K)-spaces", arXiv:1310.4035.

Identification:

The source paper asks whether every Eberlein compact space, and more generally every compact space K for which C(K) is Lindelof in its weak topology, satisfies the Bishop property. The later paper explicitly says that it answers the Eberlein question from the source paper positively: all spaces K for which C_p(K) is Lindelof have the Bishop property, and in particular all Corson and hence all Eberlein compact spaces do.

The source paper also asks whether the operators on C(K) satisfying the Bishop property always form a right, hence two-sided, ideal in B(C(K)). The later paper explicitly identifies this as Question 4.3 of the source paper and gives a negative example.

Scope:

This packet records two later-literature answers only.
