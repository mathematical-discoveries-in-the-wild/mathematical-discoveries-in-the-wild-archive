## Identification

Ozawa's introduction records the then-open case of whether the Banach algebra
of bounded operators on ell^p is amenable for p in (1,infty) except p=2.
Runde's later paper cites Ozawa, names that same p-range as the motivating
remaining case, and proves non-amenability of B(ell^p) in that open range.
Combined with the previously known endpoint cases cited there, this gives the
all-p conclusion.

This is not an original proof produced by the run. It is an exact
literature-status packet to prevent re-processing a question that was open in
2004 but answered in later literature.

## Scope

The packet clears the main introductory amenability question from Ozawa's
paper. It does not claim to settle Ozawa's separate method-side question about
whether the unit ball of ell_q projective tensor ell_p uniformly embeds into a
Hilbert space, or whether that tensor product has nontrivial cotype. Runde's
paper bypasses that route.
