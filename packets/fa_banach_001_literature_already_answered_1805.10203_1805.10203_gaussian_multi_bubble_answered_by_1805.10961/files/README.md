## Identification

The source states the Gaussian Multi-Bubble Problem and Gaussian Multi-Bubble
Conjecture as Problem 1.2 and Conjecture 1.3.  The conjecture says that
Gaussian perimeter minimizers for \(m\) prescribed Gaussian volumes are
translated simplicial Voronoi clusters.

The supporting paper proves the Gaussian Multi-Bubble Theorem: for
\(2\le q\le n+1\), simplicial clusters minimize Gaussian perimeter among
all \(q\)-clusters of prescribed positive Gaussian measures, and they are
unique up to null sets.  This directly answers the source conjecture in the
dimension range where regular simplex clusters exist.

## Provenance

The source itself notes that, after its first arXiv upload, Milman and Neeman
uploaded arXiv:1805.10961 and solved Conjecture 1.3 unconditionally.  The
Annals publication metadata and abstract independently record the same
resolution.
