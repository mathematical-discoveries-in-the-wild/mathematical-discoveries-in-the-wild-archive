This folder preserves the previous packets and attack notes after the second
consolidation.  The consolidated packet at the parent level is the current
single review entry point.

## Core Banach-Space/BCP Packets

- `packets/2311.14257_countable_capture_bcp_obstruction/`: countable capture
  obstructs BCP.
- `packets/2311.14257_countable_capture_characterization/`: quotient-line and
  common-kernel formulations of countable capture.
- `packets/2311.14257_bcp_countable_capture_exact_equivalence/`: exact theorem
  that every nonzero normed space fails BCP iff it has countable capture.
  **Do not duplicate:** this settles the qualitative BCP side for all normed
  spaces, hence for all C*-algebras as Banach spaces.  The remaining target is
  the intrinsic C*-classification and the possible BCP-to-UBCP upgrade.
- `packets/2311.14257_bcp_density_continuum_obstruction/`: BCP implies density
  at most continuum.
- `packets/2311.14257_countably_saturated_banach_capture/`: infinite-dimensional
  countably saturated Banach spaces have countable capture and fail BCP.
