## What the Proof Revealed

The negative direction for atomless von Neumann algebras is clean.  Given
countably many pure-state open sets, choose one pure state from each.  Since
all pure states on an atomless von Neumann algebra are singular, a common
projection annihilates them.  The open set `{psi: psi(p)>1/2}` then contains
none of the proposed pi-basic opens.

The atomic nonseparable cases fail by uncountably many disjoint pure-state
opens.

rank-one/transition coefficient from `xi` to `eta`.  This is exactly what the
Type I and Fell packets supply through atomic multiplier rank-one bumps.
