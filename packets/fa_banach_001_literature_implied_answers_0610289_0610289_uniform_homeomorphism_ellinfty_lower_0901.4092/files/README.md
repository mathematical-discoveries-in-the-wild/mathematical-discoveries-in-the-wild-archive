# Uniform homeomorphism of separable Banach spaces: known `ell_infinity` lower bound

Status: `literature_implied_answer (partial subcase)`

Source/open-problem paper: Valentin Ferenczi, Alain Louveau, and Christian Rosendal, *The complexity of classifying separable Banach spaces up to isomorphism*, arXiv:math/0610289.

Supporting paper: Su Gao, Steve Jackson, and Bunyamin Sari, *On the complexity of the uniform homeomorphism relation between separable Banach spaces*, arXiv:0901.4092.

## Identification

In the section on uniform homeomorphism, Ferenczi--Louveau--Rosendal ask for the Borel-reducibility complexity of uniform homeomorphism between separable Banach spaces, in particular whether it is analytic complete.

Gao--Jackson--Sari explicitly take this as the main remaining problem and prove the partial lower bound

```text
E_{\ell_\infty} <=_B uniform homeomorphism of separable Banach spaces.
```

They also prove that local equivalence of separable Banach spaces is Borel bireducible with `E_{\ell_\infty}`, so this is the strongest lower bound obtainable from Ribe/local finite-dimensional structure alone.

## Scope

This does not settle analytic completeness. Gao--Jackson--Sari explicitly note that proving `E_0^omega <=_B` uniform homeomorphism would already go beyond the local-structure barrier and would show the relation is more complex than `E_{\ell_\infty}`.
