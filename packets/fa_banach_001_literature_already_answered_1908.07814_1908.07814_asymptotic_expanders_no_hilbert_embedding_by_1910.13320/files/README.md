# Literature status: Question 7.3 of 1908.07814

Status: `literature_already_answered`.

Source problem: Kang Li, Piotr Nowak, Jan Spakula, and Jiawen Zhang,
*Quasi-local Algebras and Asymptotic Expanders*, arXiv:1908.07814.
Question 7.3 asks whether a bounded-geometry coarse disjoint union of
asymptotic expanders can coarsely embed into Hilbert space.

Answer source: Ana Khukhro, Kang Li, Federico Vigolo, and Jiawen Zhang,
*On the structure of asymptotic expanders*, arXiv:1910.13320. The authors
explicitly identify their motivating question as [21, Question 7.3] and state
that they provide a negative answer. Their Theorem B / Theorem 4.2 proves the
stronger conclusion that such a coarse disjoint union cannot coarsely embed into
any Banach space in the class `C_0`; in particular it cannot coarsely embed
into Hilbert space, and the introduction records the `L^p`, `1 <= p < infinity`,
consequence.

Scope: this packet records the negative literature answer to Question 7.3 only.
It does not claim a new proof and does not settle Question 7.1 from the source
paper. The answer paper also contains coarse Baum-Connes applications related
to Question 7.2, but those are not the focus of this packet.
