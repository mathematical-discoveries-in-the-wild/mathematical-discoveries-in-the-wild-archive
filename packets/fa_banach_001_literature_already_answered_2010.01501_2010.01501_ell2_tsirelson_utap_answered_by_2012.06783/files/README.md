# `ell_2 \oplus T^(2)` UTAP Subquestion Answered

Status: literature_already_answered (scoped subquestion only)

Source paper: Fernando Albiac, Jose L. Ansorena, and Przemyslaw Wojtaszczyk, "Uniqueness of unconditional basis of H_p(T) oplus ell_2 and H_p(T) oplus T^(2) for 0<p<1", arXiv:2010.01501.

Supporting paper: Fernando Albiac and Jose L. Ansorena, "Uniqueness of unconditional basis of ell_2 oplus T^(2)", arXiv:2012.06783.

## Identification

The source paper asks broad finite-direct-sum/UTAP questions and, near the end, lists several main remaining cases from the Bourgain--Casazza--Lindenstrauss--Tzafriri program, including whether `ell_2 oplus T^(2)` has a UTAP unconditional basis.

The supporting paper explicitly fills this gap. Its title is the exact `ell_2 oplus T^(2)` case, its abstract says that `ell_2 oplus T^(2)` has a unique unconditional basis, and its introduction says it develops new techniques to prove that case because the previous methods did not apply.

## Scope

This packet records only the `ell_2 oplus T^(2)` subquestion as already answered. It does not settle the broad question whether `X oplus Y` has a UTAP unconditional basis whenever both Banach spaces do, nor the later Gowers-square question `G^m`.
