# Positive families for the Schur weak-aleph question

Status: `partial_result_likely_valid`.

Source target: S. Gabriyelyan, J. Kakol, W. Kubis and W. Marciszewski, *Networks for the weak topology of Banach and Frechet spaces*, arXiv:1412.1748.

## Result

The packet gives affirmative subcases for Question 4.9 of the source paper:

> Let `E` be a Banach space with the Schur property satisfying conditions (i)-(iii) of Proposition 3.4. Is `E` an `aleph`-space in the weak topology?

The proved families are:

- every Banach space isomorphic to a closed subspace of `ell_1(Gamma)` with `|Gamma| <= c`;
- every weakly compactly generated Schur space, which is automatically separable and hence weakly `aleph_0`.

The first family is nonseparable when `|Gamma| = c` and extends the source theorem from `ell_1(Gamma)` itself to all its Banach subspaces.

## Scope

This is not a full solution of Question 4.9. The arbitrary Schur case remains blocked by the missing analogue of the `ell_1` annulus-localization lemma used in the source proof.
