# Literature Status: w*-SSD2P Versus SSD2P For Dual Spaces

Status: `literature_already_answered`

## Source Question

- Source paper: Rainis Haller, Jaan Kristjan Langemets, and Mart Poldvere, "Symmetric strong diameter two property", arXiv:1804.01705.
- Location: Section 6, Question 6.2, PDF page 16.
- Question: Does there exist a dual Banach space with the weak-star symmetric strong diameter two property and without the symmetric strong diameter two property?

## Supporting Answer

- Supporting paper: Rainis Haller, Jaan Kristjan Kaasik, and Andre Ostrak, "Separating diameter two properties from their weak-star counterparts in spaces of Lipschitz functions", arXiv:2404.11430.
- Location: Introduction, PDF pages 2-3, and Section 3, PDF page 6.
- Answer: Yes. The supporting paper explicitly states that it constructs the first known examples of dual Banach spaces with the `w*`-SSD2P but without the SSD2P, and says this answers arXiv:1804.01705 Question 6.2.

## Scope

This packet records an already-known literature answer, not a new proof from the run. It clears the exact source question about dual spaces. It does not address the separate later question in arXiv:2404.11430 asking whether there is a Banach space `X` with SSD2P such that `X**` lacks SSD2P.
