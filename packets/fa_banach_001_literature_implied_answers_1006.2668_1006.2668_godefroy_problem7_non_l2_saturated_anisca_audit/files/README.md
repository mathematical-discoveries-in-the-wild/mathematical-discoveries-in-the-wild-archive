# Godefroy Problem 7: Non-ell_2-Saturated Subcase Audit

Status: literature_implied_answer for a partial subcase; old packet proof invalid.

Source paper: P. Dodos, J. Lopez-Abad, and S. Todorcevic, "Banach spaces and Ramsey Theory: some open problems", arXiv:1006.2668.

Target: Godefroy's Problem 7 asks whether every separable non-Hilbertian Banach space contains countably many pairwise non-isomorphic closed infinite-dimensional subspaces.

Outcome:
- The previous packet's conclusion for the non-ell_2-saturated case is correct.
- The previous proof is not correct: minimality in the isomorphic-embeddability preorder gives mutual embeddability, not isomorphism.
- The correct proof is literature-implied by Anisca's finite-spectrum theorem, in the form recorded by Cuellar Carrera, de Rancourt, and Ferenczi: a separable Banach space with only finitely many subspace isomorphism types contains ell_2.
- Therefore every separable Banach space that is not ell_2-saturated has infinitely many pairwise non-isomorphic subspaces.
- The full Godefroy problem remains open in the located literature; the remaining case is ell_2-saturated, near Hilbert, and highly constrained by modern dichotomy results.
