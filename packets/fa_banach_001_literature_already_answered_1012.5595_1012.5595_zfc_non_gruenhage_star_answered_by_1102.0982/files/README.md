# ZFC non-Gruenhage compact space with (*)

Status: `literature_already_answered`

Source/open-problem paper: Jose Orihuela, Stanimir Troyanski, and Richard J.
Smith, *Strictly convex norms and topology*, arXiv:1012.5595.

Supporting answer paper: Richard J. Smith, *Tree Duplicates,
G_delta-diagonals and Gruenhage spaces*, arXiv:1102.0982.

## Identification

Problem 6.2 (`zfcstarnon-gru`) of arXiv:1012.5595 asks whether there is, in
ZFC, a non-Gruenhage compact space with property `(*)`.  The paper already has
a CH example, but leaves the ZFC version open.

The abstract of arXiv:1102.0982 states that it presents a ZFC example of a
locally compact, scattered, Hausdorff, non-Gruenhage space `D` with a
`G_delta`-diagonal, and explicitly says this answers a question posed by
Orihuela, Troyanski, and Smith.  In the introduction Smith recalls that every
space with a `G_delta`-diagonal has `(*)`, and that the one-point
compactification of a locally compact space with `(*)` again has `(*)`.  The
main theorem proves that the Lambda-duplicate has a `G_delta`-diagonal but is
not Gruenhage.  The following corollary states that its one-point
compactification is a scattered compact non-Gruenhage space with `(*)`.

Thus arXiv:1102.0982 explicitly answers Problem 6.2 from arXiv:1012.5595.

## Scope

This packet only records the answer to Problem 6.2, the ZFC
non-Gruenhage-with-`(*)` question.  It does not answer Problem 6.1 on
strictly convex dual norms for non-scattered compact spaces with `(*)`,
Problem 6.3 on preservation of `(*)` under continuous or perfect maps, or the
final problem about Gateaux norms for the Ostaszewski and HMVZ compact
O-spaces.
